## Title: Harven's Aliases
## APIVersion: 100030
## SavedVariables: HarvensAliases_SavedVariables
## Description: This very simple addon allows you to add an alias to any command. The command do not have to even exist. The addon should be compatible with all addons. Commands: |c00ff00/addalias|r, |c00ff00/delalias|r, |c00ff00/listaliases|r
