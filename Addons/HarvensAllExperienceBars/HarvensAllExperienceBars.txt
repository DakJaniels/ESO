## Title: Harven's All Experience Bars
## Version: 2.8.4
## Author: Harven & votan
## APIVersion: 101036 101037
## DependsOn: LibHarvensAddonSettings>=10800 LibCustomMenu>=700
## SavedVariables: HarvensAllExperienceBars_SavedVariables
## Description: This addon can display any skill/ability experience bars in your in game hud. You start with one experience bar (racial skill) which then you can move around and when you right-click on it you can add more bars from a popup menu.
